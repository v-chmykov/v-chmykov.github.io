Bows & Flowers. Illustrating Sewing Plans

Category: Illustration 30 September 19

While my dress form is still drying, I had some time to illustrate my sewing plans.

Prologue

A magical commercial or I’d better say, a short-film by Gucci inspired me to draw a couple of illustrations. That film tells a story about a man & two girls, who spend their time in mystic & foggy Venice. The city is empty, everything is blurred & tainted with gold & foggy grey colours. It’s luxurious & bohemian, as very piece that Gucci produce.

Once I watched it, I couldn’t resist wanting that blue blouse with a bow (& a beige one too). I cannot afford to buy one, but I can make my own, it won’t be Gucci, but it will tell its own story.

I didn’t have enough time to make patterns & sew it: obviously, I would also need trousers to match it. Oh, yes, and a jacket, of course, in case of a cold night. So, I decided that I could at least dress my pencil-drawn alter ego in it. That’s ok for now, as drawing is another passion of mine.
