Work with me

Well, Hello!

So, Who are you?

I am Kaya Meier, Illustrator and pattern maker of women’s clothing. In my spare time I write articles about sewing and clothing design and publish them in this blog. And at work I help clients to walk the way from the first sketch to the final pattern. To do this I discuss ideas with them, draw fashion sketches, technical drawings in real proportions and make patterns for online pattern shops and family scale manufacturers .

When should I come to you?

Clients come to me when bright sketches or detailed technical drawings are needed for communication with a tailor or a buyer. Or when a new project needs patterns for sale or sampling. It is better to reach me at the stage of the initial sketches, so that together we will think over the details, the size range and the design of the patterns. You can see the examples of the work in the portfolio.
